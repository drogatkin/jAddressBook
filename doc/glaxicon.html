About Glaxicon project

Glaxicon is P2P solution to have your contacts up-to-date, find friends, partners, customers and maybe a love. Who know?

Basic principles of Glaxicon

Complete P2P solution, there is no any centralized server, everyone can host a server. There are two types servers

  1. Connector

This type just connects interested parties to update contacts

  1. Storager

This type allows to store contacts in centralized location

Glaxicon against competitors

There are no real competitors and they can't simple be, since Glaxicon proposes  a completely new scientific approach of managing contacts.

Contact

Contact in Glaxicon is something more than a simple address book entry with bunch of e-mail, mail addresses and telephones. Contact is set of attributes attached to a person, group of person, business and so on you would like to be connected.
